About Bean Haven Coffee

Our Story

Founded in 1995 by coffee enthusiasts Maria and Giuseppe Romano, Bean Haven Coffee began as a small family-owned roastery in the heart of Bean City. What started as a passion project has grown into the community's beloved coffee destination, serving over 500 customers daily with the same commitment to quality and warmth that defined our humble beginnings.

We believe that great coffee brings people together. Every cup we serve is a testament to our dedication to ethical sourcing, expert roasting, and genuine hospitality. Our beans are sourced directly from family farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, Guatemala, and Brazil, ensuring fair wages for farmers and the finest quality for our customers.

Our Mission

"To create a welcoming space where exceptional coffee, genuine connections, and community spirit come together, one cup at a time."

Our Values

  • Quality First: We never compromise on the quality of our beans, equipment, or service
  • Ethical Sourcing: Direct trade partnerships with coffee farmers worldwide
  • Community Focus: Supporting local artists, musicians, and community events
  • Environmental Care: Sustainable practices from bean to cup
  • Continuous Learning: Our baristas undergo regular training and certification
